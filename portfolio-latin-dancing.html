Amateur Latin Competitive Dancer

Dancing has always been a part of my life. Having started when I was 7 years old, I took part in various different styles of dancing; Chinese Traditional Dancing, Jazz, Ballet, Latin, K-Pop, Hip-hop, etc. Out of all of these styles, I truly identified with Latin, it's cheeky, expressive and full of energy. When I was 15, I had no choice but to take a break to focus on my studies, there wasn't a day that went by that I didn't think about my dancing career and where I could be if I had continued. It was for that reason that I started again when I came to study in the UK - I finally had a chance to organise my time and to find a balance between work and life. Upon joining the Manchester University Ballroom and Latin Society I met my dance partner where we both began training under one of the worlds most recognised teachers Margaret Redmond. It has been a pleasure to dance with both my partner and my dance teacher, and has given me a real sense of direction and purpose in my every day life. It's very much a way of life, from the fashion, the music, the energy and certainly the people. I hope to continue my dance career for many years to come.
