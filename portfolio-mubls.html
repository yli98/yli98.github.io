Shadow Team Captain - MUBLS

When I came to the UK to study at University of Manchester in 2018, I joined the Manchester University Ballroom and Latin Society (MUBLS) to resume an all-time favourite hobby. It was there that I met my dance partner, where I was able to truly fall back in love with Latin. Over the years I've had many fantastic experiences both in and out of the society, and for that reason I decided to join the MUBLs Committee as the Shadow Team Captain. With this, I have had the opportunity to be a part of the decision-making process to enhance the experience for MUBLs and all of its members. I hope to continue providing my support and insights to better shape the future of MUBLs and pass on a truly memorable experience for all those involved.
