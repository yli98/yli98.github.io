Manchester Chinese Academy of Dance

Although my main style of dancing will always be Latin, Traditional Chinese dancing has been a diamond in the rough. It's a style that I never really connected with in my younger years, but has certainly allowed me to explore my culture in a way that I never expected I would. I appreciate the ease of learning the routines, and the different stories behind the dances. It's also given me the experience to perform in a few different shows spread across the UK, namely the Chinese New Year events in Manchester, Huddersfield, Harrogate, and Lancaster.
